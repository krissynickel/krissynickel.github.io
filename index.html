ABOUT ME

A senior at the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill pursuing a Bachelors in Computer Science with a minor in Chemistry. Always on the look out for new opportunities in technology, healthcare, or - ideally - a combination of the two. Passionate about CS education, combatting imposter syndrome within underrepresented groups in STEM, and mental health advocacy. Currently enjoying teaching (assisting) introductory computer science, counseling via Crisis Textline, and staying connected through on campus organizations including AED, a pre-health professional organization, and Phi Sigma Pi, an honors fraternity. #girlswhocode
